Corporate welfare and posture

Corporate posture and welfare: why it's an HR issue, not just a medical one

Musculoskeletal disorders caused by poor posture are the leading cause of non-acute absence in office teams in Italy and Europe. They cost companies billions of euros every year in lost productivity and turnover. Yet most welfare plans don't catch them, because they intervene after the problem occurs, not before.

LeanTil brings prevention directly to the desk. It doesn't require behavioral changes, training, or new furniture. It works in the background, collects data, and gives the company concrete metrics to act on.
